Captivity

One night in Singapore, a group of four friends named G-Dragon, Sandman, MK, and Meemo stumbled upon a mysterious bag of money. They took the money and split it four ways, knowing that they could afford to take a vacation from college for a while with the money they made. That same night, all four friends were kidnapped by an international criminal organization named the Agents of Entropy.
Fight your way through leagues of gangsters while breaking through obstacles as you make your way to the top of the hideout to escape.

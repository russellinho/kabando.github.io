Missile Command

It's the classic Missile Command game from the Atari 2600! In Missile Command, it's your job to use your missiles to destroy the enemy missiles attempting to destroy your cities. Click on the screen to fire a missile. There are 10 enemy missiles and a spaceship each level. Bonus points are awarded each level for how many cities and missiles you have left.
